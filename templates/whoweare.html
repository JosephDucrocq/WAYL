Who we are:

The idea behind WAYL has been with Joseph for quite some time. Initially limited by a lack of technical skills, he finally brought the concept to life after completing a coding bootcamp. A true music enthusiast, Joseph is drawn to intimate venues, BoilerRooms, community-driven radio stations (NTS is his go-to for work sessions), and any moment that calls for a soundtrack. One of his favorite indulgences? Cooking under the sun with a bottle of wine and some good speakers. He believes in the power of communities built around a shared passion for music. If you’re in London, you might just spot him strolling the streets, headphones on, fully immersed in sound.
