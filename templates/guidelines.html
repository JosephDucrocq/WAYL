Guidelines:

We want WAYL to be a respectful and safe community for everyone. We ask our members to respect everyone's space and privacy if they manifest such wishes. On the other hand, if you are publicaly displaying the WAYL logo, be ready to have strangers walking up to you and respectifully engage. This is why all our merch is removable. We want to give you the choice to select occasions when you are ready to engage ! Please all remeber this is a way to socialise around music, nothing more.
