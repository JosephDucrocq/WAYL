Concept :

We are driven by a deep passion for music. From coffee shops to local hangouts, offices to charity stores — music is everywhere, shaping our daily experiences. Our founders, united by this love, envisioned a simple yet powerful way to spark more meaningful interactions around it. The concept is straightforward: a visual symbol that quietly signals your connection to the community — an invitation for someone to ask, “What Are You Listening?” (Or WAYL, if you haven’t guessed yet). It’s a gentle prompt that can open the door to anything from a quick exchange to a rich, unexpected conversation. You can explore our range of products — designed to seamlessly integrate with your gear — here. Whether you're on the tube, working in a co-working space, or enjoying a sunny pint, WAYL is all about making everyday moments a little more social.
